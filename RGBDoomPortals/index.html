You wake up in a dimly lit room. There are three portals - a

red one on the left wall, a green one on the middle wall,

and a blue one on the right wall. You see a table with a

note on it. On it is a message: "Welcome, Mr. White, to your

worst nightmare! Before you are three portals, each of

which will take you somewhere different. But beware, there

is only one portal that will lead you to safety! Now, which

colour is your favourite?"


Which portal do you go through?


PORTAL PORTAL PORTAL

You step through the red portal and end up in another

room. The portal you entered closes behind you. This

room also has red, green, and blue portals. You see

another note on a table and it reads: "Are you sure this

is your favourite colour? Maybe you should try again..."


Which portal do you go through?


PORTAL PORTAL PORTAL

You step through the red portal and find yourself in the

middle of a horde of zombies. A voice speaks out and

says: "Unfortunate... Very unfortunate..." As the zombies

swarm at you, you realize that this is the end.
